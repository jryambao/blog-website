G2 looked shaky up until the end of the Rumble stage.

The only major team that lost to minor regions is G2.

Posted by JRY on May 27, 2022

G2 recently had a winning streak of 24-0 up until the end of the Rumble stage. In LEC playoffs, they went undefeated in their lower bracket run and claimed the LEC trophy once again after rebuilding their team in 2022. G2 also went undefeated in the MSI group stage going up against the minor regions.

Every one thought that G2 is a potential MSI winner due to taking down two of the best teams in the tournament at the same day, T1 & RNG. But after Day 2 of the Rumble stage, they went on a 5 losing streak, losing 3 of those games were from the minor regions. They were the only major team to lose against the minor region in the tournament.

G2 eventually ended their last match of the Rumble stage as a win

The final standings of MSI Rumble stage

All of us thought that EG would win against G2 considering their form is faltering but G2 once again proved that EU is better than NA. G2 ended the rumble stage with a 5-5 record. Since RNG chose EG in the semi-finals match-up, G2 will take on T1, who also went undefeated in their entire split.
