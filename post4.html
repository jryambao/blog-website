RNG is looking for their third MSI trophy

After defeating EG, RNG once again reached MSI finals after winning last year and in 2018.

Posted by JRY on May 28, 2022

After winning MSI 2021, RNG is once again looking to win the tournament. They went 8-2 in the rumble stage losing only games to G2 and T1 In sweep of Evil Geniuses, RNG have qualified once again to the finals event of MSI. They will go up against either T1 or G2, who will play the semi finals match up on May 28, 2022.

While Evil Geniuses did put up a good fight in game 2 & 3, RNG was strong enough to prevent them to have a comeback attempt from the North american team. RNG won the first game ending it on a stomp. After defeating the North American team, RNG will get a chance to defend their MSI throne and be hailed the Kings of MSI if they win the trophy, leaving them as the only team to win three MSI trophies.
