T1 and G2 rekindle their fiery rivalry in MSI 2022 semi-finals.

Will we see the revenge of T1 against their kryptonite or will G2 triumph again?

Posted by Start Bootstrap on August 24, 2022

Let's take a look back to their rivalry that started on 2019. MSI 2019, T1 were one of the favorites to win the tournament however they failed to capture the trophy since they lost to G2 in the semi finals matchup. In Worlds 2019, they got match up again with G2 in the semi finals and once again, they failed to defeat the European team. Now in MSI 2022, with a whole new different team, T1 and G2 will go up against each other rekindling the old rivalry That once started in 2019.

T1 in 2022 seems a different beast than in 2019, going undefeated in their LCK spring split with a record of 20-0 including the LCK finals. All of T1 players are mechanically monster, they all excel at their own roles and is considered already one of the best going throughout the year even though they haven't played one whole year yet. G2 on the other hand rebuilt their team, keeping only two players which is Caps and Jankos after having a dissapointing 2021 year which also is touted as a super team.

The matchup to watch for this series would be the midlane. Faker and Caps going against each other once again, two of the best mid laner to do it. Faker who is considered the greatest of all time versus Caps who is considered the best western mid laner of all time. The last time they met was in the Rumble stage and T1 managed to win against G2 by baiting them into going to the baron pit, T1 had problems setting up the baron during the tournament and they've lost a considerable amount of games due to them flipping the baron. Little did G2 know, T1 had set a trap for them and killing all of their players and ending the game in 21 minutes.
